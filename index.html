What is the App?

This App allows students to find or give tutoring classes within their school. The principal advantage of this App is that finding a tutoring lesson or finding a student is very fast and easy: you can find or give a lesson in one or two days!

How to register?

The first time you open the App, you land on a login/register page. If you don't already have an account you have to register. After clicking on the register button, the app is going to ask you whether you want to use the app as a “student”, who wants to find tutoring lessons, or a “tutor”, who wants to give tutoring lessons.

If you choose “student” —--> you will have to fill in a quick form with basic information such as your essec email address (only essec emails work), your programm (BBA or PGE), the subjects you are the most interested in…

If you choose “tutor”----> you will also have to fill in your ESSEC mail. You will then receive an email inviting you to an interview with one of our team members. You will be asked to bring your ESSEC card and your grades in the course you want to teach.

How to use?

-For the “student” user:

Once logged in you arrive on the home page. (define what is on the home page) To book a session, the user has to click the “book a course” button. He will then land on a page with a calendar displaying the different sessions with the name of the tutor, the duration of the course as well as the number of seats available, depending on the day. There would also be a “sort by” tab that would give the possibility to the user to display only the course he is interested in on the calendar.

If the user doesn’t find a session that suits his availability, he can click the “ask for a session” button: he would then indicate the day, time, subject and duration of the session he wants to have. The tutor users would have this session displayed in their calendar as well as a notification saying “[Name] asks for a session”.

-For the “tutor” user:

The user registered as a tutor would also have access to this calendar, but could place his availability there, whether recurrent or not. He/she could indicate several information about the session such as the maximum number of students he/she would like, the place…

The “tutor” user also has the possibility to turn on or off notifications of people asking for sessions (mentioned above).

The app can create a groupchat with the “Tutor” and the “students” to communicate timing, classroom and other important information for the tutoring class to take place.

The “tutor” can also receive opinions from students regarding the course and their method of teaching so that other students know how they work and search for the adequate “tutor” for them depending on how they learn, the number of students in the group and the subject taught.
